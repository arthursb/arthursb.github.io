# Who am I?
A Brazilian software developer who works at Instituto Atlântico making applications that employ 3D graphics and/or augmented reality to assist our clients. MSc Computer Science graduate at Universidade Federal do Ceará. Research and development interests include games, mobile apps, AR/VR/XR/MR, and data visualization.

## Previous Experience
- **Game Developer** at [Advance Comunicação](https://advance.com.br/) (2016 - 2018): I worked mainly as a game developer. We developed a puzzle game for iOS and Android called 2001: Space Puzzle. We also developed games under request and other in-house tools. It was my first experience developing and releasing a commercial game. 
- **Software Developer** at [Crearetech](https://www.facebook.com/crearetech/) (2015): I worked on a budget shooting simulator for the local police. This simulator used mixed reality to cheapen expenses with training. 
- **Game Developer and Researcher** at [GEM Lab](https://gem.cs.dal.ca/) (2014): under the supervision of Dr. Derek Reilly, my team and I developed a mixed reality game called Beach Pong. It was presented in an art event called Nocturne. We produced two research papers, but the best thing for me was seeing many people gathering around to play the game.

## Education
- **Computer Science** (2018 - 2021): I achieved my MSc Computer Science degree at [Universidade Federal do Ceará (UFC)](http://www.ufc.br/). I wrote a thesis on Games User Research and how user testing and data visualization improved a 2D game.
- **Mobile App Development** (2018 - 2020): Finished a specialization course at [Centro Universitário 7 de Setembro](https://www.uni7.edu.br/). I studied Android and iOS development, both in native and hybrid environments. During this time, I researched the usage of dark patterns in the UX design of Brazilian food delivery mobile apps.
- **Digital Systems and Media** (2012 - 2017) : I graduated from UFC and focused my studies on game design. I developed an immersive mixed reality game using Kinect as my final project. The research was given a [Best Paper Award](http://usuarios.upf.br/~rieder/svr2017/awards.html) in 2017.
- **Computer Science** (2014): I've spent one year abroad thanks to an exchange program called Science without Borders. I've studied at [Dalhousie University](https://www.dal.ca/). During this time, I also worked at the [Graphics and Experimental Media Laboratory](https://gem.cs.dal.ca/).
